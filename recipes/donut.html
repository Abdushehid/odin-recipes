Soft Sugar Donuts

Description

These halal donuts are light, fluffy, and covered with a sweet glaze. Perfect with tea or coffee

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Warm milk
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Eggs
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Mix warm milk, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for 10 mins.
  2. Add flour, eggs, and butter. Knead the dough and let it rise.
  3. Roll out and cut the dough into donut shapes.
  4. Fry the donuts in hot oil until golden.
  5. Coat with sugar or glaze and let cool.
