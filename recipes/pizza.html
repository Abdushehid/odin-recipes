Spicy Halal Pizza

Description

This halal pizza is made with delicious chicken, fresh veggies, and melted cheese. A perfect meal for family or friends!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough or base
  • Pizza sauce
  • Halal grilled chicken
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Onion slices

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C.
  2. Spread sauce over the pizza base.
  3. Add chicken and vegetables on top.
  4. Sprinkle cheese and seasonings.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes.
